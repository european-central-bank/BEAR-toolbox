
1. Open the instant zero restrictions table, and enter your restrictions.

2. Save and close the Excel file.

3. Choose the other settings in the form below.

4. Press the Submit button to apply the settings.

!!! warning "Maximum number of zero restrictions"

    At most **n(n−1)/2 − 1** exact zero restrictions are allowed, where **n** is the number of endogenous variables (e.g. for n=3 → max 2 zeros, for n=4 → max 5, for n=5 → max 9).

    If you want exactly n(n−1)/2 zeros arranged in a triangular pattern (fully recursive identification), use the **Cholesky** identifier instead - it is faster, deterministic, and gives the same answer.
